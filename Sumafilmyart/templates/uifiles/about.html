Mission

Sumafilmyarts mission is feature films and ultimate goal is to be a world renowned, multi-faceted movie studio that revolutionizes the way films are financed and consumed, while inspiring talented, undiscovered artists to achieve their specific goals.

Suma Kanakala is an award-winning television presenter, actress, and producer who primarily works in Telugu television. Her humour and ability to combine modernity with tradition have earned her a plethora of admirers in her 30-year-long career, making her widely popular. Despite not being born Telugu, her ability to express herself eloquently, combined with her wit and engaging personality has seen her become one of the most respected presenters in the TV industry.

Our Brief

Suma Filmy Arts is one of the top production houses in the television industry. Led by Suma Kanakala, renowned Indian television presenter and producer, our debut production, the gameshow Lakku Kikku, was a critical hit. Energised by the success, more such innovative ideas were nurtured under the wisdom of Suma Filmy Arts culminating in TV shows Genes, F3, and Bhale Chance le, a comedy web series - House of Hungama and entertainment channels Jujube TV & Suma Akka, which translated into an atmosphere of unbound joy in millions of households.

Our Founder

Armed with vast experience in the Tv industry, technical intricacies, and by now legendary humour, Suma debuted as a producer in 2012 with the hit show Lukku Kikku which also featured her as the host delivering to the audience a new and engaging experience.

Inspired by the success, suma went on to become one of the leading producers in the Telugu TV industry, ensuring she is the source of Joy even when she is not hosting.
