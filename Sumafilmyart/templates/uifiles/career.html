  • Collaborate with other writers, directors, and producers to bring projects to life
  • Collaborate with other writers, directors, and producers to bring projects to life
  • Conduct research to ensure accuracy and authenticity in storytelling
  • Edit and revise written material as necessary
  • Stay up-to-date with industry trends and emerging technologies to continually improve your craft

  • Strong attention to detail and ability to work under tight deadlines
  • A passion for storytelling and visual media

This is a full-time job opportunity for creative individuals who are passionate about storytelling and have a strong sense of visual media. If you are a talented writer looking for an exciting opportunity to work with a dynamic and growing production company, then we encourage you to apply today.
